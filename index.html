Travel to Paris

Location

Paris is located in northern central France, in a north-bending arc of the river Seine whose crest includes two islands, the Île Saint-Louis and the larger Île de la Cité, which form the oldest part of the city.

Views

  • Eiffel Tower
  • The Louvre
  • The Arc de Triomphe

Eiffel Tower

Eiffel Tower is a construction of Gustave Eiffel for the 1889 Universal Exposition

The Louvre

The Louvre is one of the world's largest museums and a historic monument

The Arc de Triomphe

The Arc de Triomphe is a monument at the center of the Place de l'Étoile, commemorating the victories of France and honoring those who died in battle
